Sel Roti

Description

Sel Roti is a traditional Nepali rice-based ring-shaped bread, often prepared during festivals and special occasions like Dashain and Tihar. It has a slightly sweet flavor, crispy exterior, and soft interior. This festive delicacy is usually enjoyed with tea or paired with fried vegetables and meat dishes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice (soaked overnight)
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2–3 tbsp ghee or butter
  • ½ cup milk (or water as needed)
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. Drain the soaked rice and grind it with milk to make a smooth batter.
  2. Add sugar, ghee, and cardamom powder; mix until well combined.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan.
  4. Pour the batter in a circular ring shape into the hot oil.
  5. Fry until golden brown on both sides, turning gently.
  6. Remove and drain excess oil before serving warm
